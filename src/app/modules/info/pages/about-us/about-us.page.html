Our Mission

ASCENDynamics is a non-for-profit organization that incubates worker-owned cooperatives. Our mission is to help entrepreneurs create successful startups by providing technical, marketing, development, funding, and networking resources. We believe that worker-owned cooperatives are an important model for economic and social justice, and we are committed to supporting their growth and success. We also share SEO and digital outreach strategies so that cooperatives can expand their online visibility.

Our Values

At ASCENDynamics, we believe in the power of cooperation and collaboration. We value equity, inclusion, sustainability, and innovation, and we strive to embody these values in all that we do. We are committed to creating a better world, and we believe that worker-owned cooperatives are a critical part of that vision.
