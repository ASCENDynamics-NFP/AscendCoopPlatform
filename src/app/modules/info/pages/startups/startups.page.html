Startup Incubator

Our Startup Incubator program mentors and supports entrepreneurs launching worker-owned cooperative businesses. We provide end-to-end assistance – from technical development and business planning to securing funding and building partnerships. The incubator is especially committed to founders from underserved communities, ensuring they have the guidance and resources to build sustainable, community-owned startups. With ASCENDynamics' mentorship and tools (including training in SEO and digital outreach), new cooperatives can get off the ground and thrive.
