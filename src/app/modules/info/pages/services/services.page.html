Technical Assistance

We help nonprofits and cooperative startups overcome technical challenges with hands-on support. Our team offers training workshops, open-source tools, and one-on-one IT consulting to ensure organizations can leverage technology effectively. From setting up collaborative software to building an online presence, we provide the technical assistance needed to strengthen your mission.

Development Support

From fundraising to organizational development, we guide you in growing your impact. Our development support includes assistance with grant writing, strategic planning, and building sustainable revenue models. We help nonprofits and cooperatives access funding opportunities and refine their business plans, positioning them to scale and serve their communities better.

Marketing Support
