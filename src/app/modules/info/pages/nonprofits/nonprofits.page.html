Empowering Nonprofits to Make a Difference

ASCENDynamics is here to help nonprofits increase their impact.

Collaborative Platform

We're currently developing an open-source, web-based collaboration platform tailored towards organizations, movements, nonprofits, and collaborative groups. This platform aims to enhance capacity for collaboration, streamline internal organization, track and leverage volunteer contributions, manage donations, facilitate internal and external communications, and amplify societal impact. You can follow the development of this platform on our GitHub page.

We invite you to join our Slack community where you can connect and contribute with other volunteers working on the platform.
