Our Team

Our team is a passionate group of innovators, volunteers, and leaders dedicated to social impact. Together, we combine expertise in software development, nonprofit management, and community organizing to drive ASCENDynamics' mission forward. We are proud to be a volunteer-powered organization – our board members and contributors donate their time and skills to support cooperative innovation and nonprofit collaboration.

Board & Leadership

The ASCENDynamics board of directors provides strategic vision and guidance and ensures we stay true to our mission. Our leadership is hands-on and volunteer-driven, with deep commitment to the communities we serve.

 Volunteers

Volunteers are the heart of ASCENDynamics. Our volunteer network includes software engineers who contribute to our open-source platform, project managers who coordinate programs, and activists who lead workshops and outreach. We track volunteer engagement and celebrate each individual's contributions, because we know our success is a direct result of their dedication. If you're looking to make a meaningful difference, joining our volunteer team is a great way to apply your talents to a worthy cause.
