About the Think Tank

The ASCENDynamics Think Tank is our innovation hub for envisioning a better future. It's a space for visionary thinking, strategizing, and problem-solving around the challenges faced by cooperatives and nonprofits. We bring together community leaders, volunteers, and experts to explore creative solutions for economic empowerment and social justice. In our Think Tank, big ideas flourish – from new cooperative business models to SEO research that helps grassroots movements amplify their voice online. Everything we learn and develop here feeds back into our platform and community, fueling continuous improvement and inspiration.

Research

Our Think Tank spearheads research into topics that matter for cooperative and nonprofit success. We publish insights on worker-owned cooperative development, effective nonprofit collaboration strategies, and technological innovations for social impact. Recent research topics include open-source software tools for volunteer coordination, digital marketing strategies for mutual aid groups, and case studies on successful cooperative enterprises. All our research and publications are openly shared, providing a knowledge base that anyone can use to further their own mission. By turning research into action, we help grassroots organizations stay informed and empowered with the best practices available.

Events & Workshops

Innovation doesn't happen in isolation – that's why our Think Tank hosts events, workshops, and webinars to bring people together. Join hackathons, panel discussions, and collaborative workshops where like-minded nonprofit professionals, tech enthusiasts, and community organizers brainstorm and build new solutions. For example, you might participate in a hackathon to develop an open-source volunteer tracking app, or attend a roundtable on improving nonprofit outreach through technology. These events are designed to spark collaboration and let participants share expertise.

Participate in our next Think Tank event to network with changemakers and contribute your ideas. Together, we'll transform bold concepts into real-world initiatives that uplift our communities.
