Our Mission

At ASCENDynamics, our mission is to incubate worker-owned cooperatives and enhance collaboration among nonprofit organizations and mutual aid groups. We help entrepreneurs and community leaders turn their ideas into successful ventures by providing technical development, marketing guidance, funding strategies, and networking opportunities. By championing cooperative business models and open collaboration, we advance economic and social justice, especially in underserved communities that benefit most from empowerment and equitable growth.

Our Values

We believe lasting change comes from working together. Our core values guide everything we do:

  • Cooperation & Community: We foster a culture of teamwork where nonprofits, cooperatives, volunteers, and donors share knowledge and resources to achieve common goals.
  • Inclusion & Equity: We uplift diverse voices and focus on underserved communities, ensuring everyone has access to opportunities and a seat at the table.
  • Innovation & Open Source: We embrace creativity and technology, using open-source tools and ideas to drive social innovation and improve how organizations collaborate.
  • Sustainability & Impact: We commit to solutions that are socially, economically, and environmentally sustainable, aiming for tangible social impact and a more just world.

If our mission and values resonate with you, we welcome you to be part of our journey. Whether you're a volunteer ready to contribute, a nonprofit seeking partnership, or a donor looking to make a difference, there's a place for you at ASCENDynamics. Join us in creating a cooperative future where everyone can thrive.
