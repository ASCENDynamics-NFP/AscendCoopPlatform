Child Safety Standards Our commitment to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE)

Our Commitment

ASCENDynamics NFP Platform is committed to providing a safe environment for all users, with special attention to protecting minors from child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

Safety Measures

User Verification

All users must verify their email addresses and agree to our community guidelines

Content Moderation

Active monitoring and moderation of user-generated content using both automated systems and human review

Reporting System

Easy-to-use in-app reporting tools for users to report inappropriate content or behavior

Zero Tolerance Policy

Immediate suspension and reporting to authorities for any CSAE or CSAM violations

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report child safety concerns through multiple channels:

  • In-App Reporting: Report button available on all user profiles and content
  • Email: support@ascendynamicsnfp.org
  • Emergency Contact: For immediate threats, users are directed to contact local law enforcement

Response Procedures

  1. Immediate Action: Suspected CSAE/CSAM content is immediately removed and accounts suspended
  2. Investigation: Thorough review by our safety team within 24 hours
  3. Law Enforcement: Reporting to NCMEC (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children) and relevant authorities
  4. Documentation: Maintaining records as required by law for potential legal proceedings

Legal Compliance

ASCENDynamics NFP Platform complies with all applicable laws regarding child safety, including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • State and federal laws regarding mandatory reporting
  • International laws in jurisdictions where our service is available

Age Requirements

Our platform requires users to be at least 13 years of age. For users under 18, we implement additional safety measures including enhanced privacy settings and restricted communication features.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly review and update our child safety measures in consultation with safety experts, law enforcement, and child protection organizations. Our policies are reviewed annually and updated as needed to address emerging threats.

Contact Information

For questions about our child safety standards or to report concerns:

  • Safety Team: support@ascendynamicsnfp.org
  • General Contact: support@ascendynamicsnfp.org
