How We Help

ASCENDynamics offers a variety of services to support worker-owned cooperatives and nonprofits.

Technical Assistance

Our technical assistance services are designed to help startups and nonprofits overcome technical challenges and develop the skills they need to succeed. We offer training, consulting, and other support services to help our clients leverage technology to achieve their goals.

Development Support

Our development support services are designed to help startups and nonprofits grow and scale their operations. We offer fundraising, grant writing, and other support services to help our clients access the resources they need to achieve their goals.

Marketing Support

Our marketing support services are designed to help startups and nonprofits reach their target audience and build their brand. We offer branding, digital marketing, social media management, and other support services to help our clients tell their story and connect with their community.

Networking Resources

Our networking resources are designed to help startups and nonprofits connect with other organizations, potential partners, and funders. We offer networking events, matchmaking services, and other support services to help our clients build their networks and expand their reach.
